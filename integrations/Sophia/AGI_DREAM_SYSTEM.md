## AGI Enhancements Explained

### 1. GPT-5 Meta-Cognitive Analysis

**What it does:**
- Understands your current life context (career, relationships, goals)
- Interprets dreams in relation to your specific situation
- Identifies hidden psychological dynamics beyond symbol matching
- Provides actionable insights tailored to your journey

**Example:**
```
Basic: "Snake represents transformation"

AGI: "The snake in your dream represents your anxiety about the career 
transition you mentioned. It's not threatening you - it's guiding you 
toward the new opportunity (the door). Your unconscious is telling you 
that this change, while scary, is actually a path to growth. The snake's 
non-threatening nature suggests you have the inner resources to handle 
this transformation."
```

### 2. Consciousness State Correlation

**What it does:**
- Compares dream emotional tone with waking consciousness
- Detects shadow manifestations (repressed emotions)
- Identifies compensation patterns
- Suggests integration opportunities

**Example:**
```
Waking state: Calm, confident about career decision
Dream state: Anxious, lost in dark office

AGI Insight: "Your dream reveals anxiety about the career change that 
you're not acknowledging in waking life. This is shadow material - your 
unconscious is processing fears you've repressed. Integration opportunity: 
Consciously acknowledge and work through these anxieties."
```

### 3. Adaptive Pattern Learning

**What it does:**
- Stores each dream in episodic memory
- After 10+ dreams, consolidates to semantic memory
- Learns YOUR personal symbol meanings (not just universal)
- Builds confidence in interpretations over time

**Example:**
```
After 1 dream: "Water appears (universal: unconscious)"
After 5 dreams: "Water appears frequently in your dreams"
After 10 dreams: "Water is YOUR personal symbol for emotional processing. 
It appears when you're dealing with relationship challenges. Confidence: 85%"
```

### 4. Lumen Philosophical Grounding

**What it does:**
- Maps dreams to three dimensions of Being:
  - **Onticum**: Existence, identity, being
  - **Structurale**: Order, form, structure
  - **Participatum**: Connection, relationship, participation
- Measures balance across dimensions
- Provides philosophical insights

**Example:**
```
Dream scores:
- Onticum: 0.8 (high) - Existential themes about career identity
- Structurale: 0.7 (high) - Archetypal structure present
- Participatum: 0.4 (low) - Limited relational content

AGI Insight: "Your dreams are heavily focused on Being and Structure, 
but lacking in Connection. Consider how this career transition affects 
your relationships. Seek balance by engaging more with others."
```

### 5. Temporal Pattern Recognition

**What it does:**
- Tracks symbols across multiple dreams
- Identifies narrative arcs
- Measures sequence coherence
- Detects recurring themes

**Example:**
```
Dream 1: Lost in office â†’ anxiety
```

## Troubleshooting

### GPT-5 API Errors

**Problem**: 429 Rate Limit
**Solution**: Reduce analysis frequency or upgrade API tier

**Problem**: Invalid API key
**Solution**: Verify OPENAI_API_KEY is set correctly

### Voice System Issues

**Problem**: No spoken analysis
**Solution**: Check GEMINI_API_KEY and enable_voice=True

**Problem**: Voice quality poor
**Solution**: Try different voice types (NOVA, ALLOY, etc.)

### Learning Not Working

**Problem**: No personal patterns emerging
**Solution**: Need 10+ dreams for consolidation

**Problem**: Low confidence scores
**Solution**: Provide more detailed dream descriptions
